About Melanie Broadley

Diverse approaches to suit any circumstance

Hi, I'm Mel, and I am a counsellor who understands what it means to be neurodivergent because I am neurodivergent too. I specialize in working with adults and teenagers who think and experience the world differently. I believe that just because your brain is wired differently, does not that mean you are broken or need to be fixed. Traditional therapy does not always get that, but I do! My approach is all about understanding your unique mind and finding ways to work with it, not against it.
